Small Pet Habitat Guide

Creating the Perfect Home

Small pets need ample space to exercise, explore, and express natural behaviors. Minimum cage sizes are just the starting point!

Space Matters

The bigger the habitat, the happier your pet. Always choose the largest enclosure you can accommodate.

Multi-Level Living

Add platforms, ramps, and tunnels to maximize vertical space and encourage exploration.

Cozy Hideouts

Every small pet needs at least one secure hiding spot where they can feel safe and relaxed.

Regular Cleaning

Spot clean daily and do a full habitat clean weekly to maintain a healthy environment.

Minimum Habitat Sizes

Hamsters

450+ sq inches floor space

Guinea Pigs

7.5+ sq ft for one, 10.5+ for two

Rabbits

12+ sq ft, plus daily exercise time

Ferrets

Multi-level cage, minimum 24"x24"x18"
