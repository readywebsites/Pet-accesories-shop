Cat Care Essentials

Nutrition Needs

Cats are obligate carnivores requiring high-protein diets with taurine. Choose food specifically formulated for their life stage.

Comfort & Rest

Cats sleep 12-16 hours daily. Provide cozy beds, perches, and hiding spots to satisfy their need for security and observation.

Play & Exercise

Interactive toys prevent boredom and obesity. Rotate toys regularly to keep your cat mentally stimulated and physically active.

Grooming & Health

Regular brushing reduces hairballs. Provide scratching posts to maintain claw health and prevent furniture damage.
