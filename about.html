How PetJoy Began

PetJoy was founded in 2023 by animal lovers Sarah Chen and Miguel Rodriguez, who noticed a gap in the pet market. While there were plenty of functional pet products, few focused on bringing genuine joy to both pets and their owners.

After adopting their rescue dog, Sunny, and cat, Luna, they struggled to find toys and accessories that were both high-quality and truly engaging. Everything seemed either too basic or overly complicated.

That's when the idea for PetJoy was born: a one-stop shop for pet supplies that prioritize happiness, playfulness, and the special bond between pets and their people.

"We believe every wag, purr, and chirp deserves to be celebrated. That's why we created PetJoy - to spread happiness, one pet at a time."

- Sarah & Miguel, Founders

Our Mission & Values

Pet Happiness First

Every product we carry is tested and approved by real pets. If it doesn't bring joy, it doesn't make our shelves.

Sustainability Matters

We're committed to eco-friendly products and packaging. Joy shouldn't come at the expense of our planet.

Community Focused

We support local shelters and rescues. 5% of every purchase goes to pet welfare organizations.

Quality Guaranteed

We rigorously test all products for safety, durability, and fun-factor. Your satisfaction is our promise.
