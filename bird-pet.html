Bird Size & Cage Guide

Choosing the Right Cage

Birds need space to fly, stretch, and play. The cage should be at least 1.5 times your bird's wingspan in width, depth, and height.

Parakeets / Budgies
18" x 18" x 24" minimum
Cockatiels / Lovebirds
24" x 24" x 30" minimum
Conures / Small Parrots
30" x 30" x 36" minimum
African Greys / Amazons
36" x 36" x 48" minimum
Download Complete Guide

Essential Bird Care Tips

Cage Placement

Place cage in a family area but away from drafts, direct sunlight, and kitchens. Birds are social and need interaction.

Nutrition

Offer a varied diet of pellets, fresh vegetables, fruits, and limited seeds. Always provide clean, fresh water.

Mental Stimulation

Rotate toys weekly to prevent boredom. Foraging toys encourage natural behaviors and mental exercise.

Health Check

Regular vet visits, wing/nail trims, and monitoring droppings are essential for early illness detection.
